Step 3: The Thumbaround Harmonic

You made it! This is the final stretch. Now that you have the thumbaround and its reverse counterpart, you are ready to learn the Thumbaround Harmonic. At the end of this video you will be on your way to becoming a pro spinner!

The ThumbAround Harmonic is a pen-spinning trick that combines the ThumbAround and the Reverse ThumbAround in a continuous motion. It involves executing a ThumbAround, catching the pen, and immediately performing a Reverse ThumbAround in succession. This trick likely developed as spinners experimented with fluid transitions between moves, seeking to create seamless and rhythmic loops in their combos. The harmonic nature of the trick makes it visually appealing and serves as a foundation for more advanced sequences. As pen spinning evolved, the ThumbAround Harmonic became a staple in freestyle routines, helping spinners refine their control and timing while maintaining a smooth flow.
