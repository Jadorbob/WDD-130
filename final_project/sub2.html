Step 2: The Reverse Thumbaround

The Reverse Thumbaround is exactly as it sounds. The thumbaround, but backwards! This trick is heavily used in penspinning, at the entry, moderate and advanced levels. I learned it right after learning the thumbaround. The two tricks go hand in hand.

The Reverse ThumbAround is a fundamental pen-spinning trick that evolved as a natural counterpart to the original ThumbAround. While the standard ThumbAround propels the pen in a forward motion around the thumb, the Reverse ThumbAround spins it in the opposite direction, requiring a different flick and catch technique. This trick likely emerged as pen spinners sought to expand their repertoire and create smoother transitions between moves. It gained popularity in online pen-spinning communities and tutorials, becoming an essential building block for advanced combos. Mastering the Reverse ThumbAround is crucial for developing fluidity in freestyle spinning and unlocking more complex trick variations.
