Home page

Welcome to my website! This page is dedicated to teaching the average beginner the fundamentals and advanced technique for the Penspinning thumbaround Harmonic. I will be utilizing the Channel tutorials from famous youtube channel: Pen Stock!

Images for the Home page

Thumbaround

In this lesson we will go over how to do a basic thumbaround. This step is essential for the finished product, it provides the backbone for the trick and will give you the necessary experience for future steps.

Reverse thumbaround

In the second lesson we will learn basically what we learned in the first video. This is going to take some practice, but now that you have lesson one down, lesson two will be easier! Click the video to learn how to do it!
